About

Quantum Computing is a Research Group that brings together students and researchers from all over the country, focused on areas such as Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence and High Performance Computing. Our group acts as a Central Hub for other groups and centers worldwide, encompassing quantum technologies, quantum metrology, superconductivity, nanomaterials, optics and quantum information. We drive scientific progress through multidisciplinary research and international cooperation.

Interesting fact

  • First Research Group in Quantum Computing at the Universidad Nacional de Colombia.
  • The largest research group in the country.
  • Associated with 3 campus, 5 faculties and +10 academic programs.
