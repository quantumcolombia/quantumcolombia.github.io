Quantum Optimization

Solutions to related optimization problems, using algorithmic techniques, such as quantum mapping, quantum memories, quantum machine learning and variational optimization, such as: Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA), Quantum Annealing or Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE).

Quantum Machine Learning

Quantum Machine Learning is a promising application for quantum computers, with potential for computer vision, natural language processing, general pattern search in large data sets, measurements, and density estimation.

Quantum Simulation

Quantum computers promise to be a very powerful tool for solving a wide range of simulation problems, from modeling complex physical systems and states of matter to analyzing chemical compounds, pharmaceuticals, and materials science.

Quantum Chemistry

Quantum chemistry research includes the development and application of quantum dynamics methods for studies of reaction dynamics in excited states and quantum control in polyatomic systems. Applications focus on semiconductor materials, protein synthesis and catalytic reactions.

Quantum Error Correction

Quantum Error Correction (QEC) is a technique for protecting quantum information from errors caused by decoherence and other quantum perturbations. Using quantum codes, QEC enables error detection and correction without directly measuring the quantum state, preserving quantum coherence and data integrity. This technology is crucial for the development of noisy intermediate scale quantum computers (NISQ).

Quantum Metrology

To improve the sensitivity and precision of measurements, quantum metrology takes advantage of entanglement and superposition to estimate physical quantities more accurately, with applications in, for example, atomic clocks, GPS navigation and satellite communication, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) by improving their sensitivity and resolution, and the detection of gravitational waves.
